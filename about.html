Biography


Jack Yang earned his Bachelor’s degree in Actuarial Science from Baruch College and a Full Stack Developer certificate from Columbia University only two and a half years after high school. In addition to his study in the US, he also completed a semester abroad at Yonsei University in Seoul, South Korea.


Recently, he has obtained his master’s degree in Business Analytics and is working as a Software Developer at USEReady, an IT service management company that provides Business Intelligence (BI) solutions leveraging cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) and harness the power of Collective Intelligence (CI) to unlock the power of data.


Prior to joining USEReady, Jack has worked as a Legal and Technical Analyst at Law Firm of Yuan Jiang, P.C. With the help of senior attorneys and the latest technology applications, he has produced the law firm's own E-Forms with protected restrict format for discovery and filing at the County, State, and Federal Court levels. He has also reconstructed the law firm's website using modern web design techniques. The new website is mobile-responsive and user-centered, which includes advanced features such as blog posting, live chatting, and online appointment booking.


Whether working on academic or professional projects, Jack always aims to go above and beyond the requirements by utilizing his data-analytics and problem-solving skills, which he will leverage into his future career as an expert market analyst who can transfer digital information into a catalyst for business success.

Global Blockchain Hackathon - Coding/Developer Team

• Identified problems:
- Organizations such as ICO, NGO, Churches, Kickstarters, and Crowdsourcing startups maintain their own secret ledgers of actual receivables and payments.
- Donators/Investors may never understand how the proceeds are being used.
- Without public scrutiny, proceeds and expenses have a very high degree of risk.
• Suggested solutions:
- By integrating bank records and storing data to a permissioned blockchain, Aud.it creates transparent records and real-time funds tractability of donations, expenses, and contracts with vendors, which removes the possibility of fraud and simplifies compliance regulations.
• Created an application that provides:
- Real-time transactions update, modular design, and payment auditing
- Monitoring and validation of transcations, blocks, and small constracts
- Notification of user non-compliance
- Permission based access using Hyperledger Fabric
- Supervision and validation of remediation
- Enforcement of consensus
Team Members: Yvonne Wang, Martin Huang, Randall Huynh, Joel Labelle

Front & Back End Developer

• Identified problems:
- EDM fanatics lack a unified tool to plan for EDM events.
- They often need to jump between multiple websites and apps to arrange different aspects of their trips.
